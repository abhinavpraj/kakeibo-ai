# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, identity, or personal characteristics.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are encouraged to:

* Be respectful and professional.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Be considerate of differing viewpoints.
* Collaborate in good faith.
* Help create a positive learning environment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Trolling or disruptive conduct.
* Publishing private information without consent.
* Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled appropriately.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, contributions, or participants whose behavior violates this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* Project discussions
* Issues
* Pull requests
* Community interactions related to KakeiboAI
